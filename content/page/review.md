
## What Makes a Movie 'Good'?

A good film is a film that achieves what it sets out to communicate to the audience. This is a simple definition, but it is a good one. A film that is trying to be a comedy should be funny, a film that is trying to be a drama should be moving, and a film that is trying to be a horror should be scary.

However, each film does not exist in a vacuum. A film is a product of its time, its culture, and its creators. All parts evolve independently and inform the films made after them.

## My Review Process

When I analyze a film there are two broad traits that I consider: the film's intellectual value and its emotional value.
